class nansat.geolocationarray.GeolocationArray(xVRT=None, yVRT=None, xBand=1, yBand=1, srs='', lineOffset=0, lineStep=1, pixelOffset=0, pixelStep=1, dataset=None)¶

Container for GEOLOCATION ARRAY data

Keeps references to bands with X and Y coordinates, offset and step of pixel and line. All information is stored in dictionary self.d

Instance of GeolocationArray is used in VRT and ususaly created in a Mapper.
